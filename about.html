Learn about Kickin Burgers

Quick Facts

  • Restaurant Created: 2001
  • Owner: Aaliyah Thomas
  • Food Served: Burgers & Fries
  • Motto: "Home of the Juciest Burgers!"
  • Meat Used: 73% Lean Ground Beef

Welcome to Kickin Burgers, a beloved burger joint that has been serving up mouthwatering delights since 2001. Founded by Aaliyah Thomas, Kickin Burgers is more than just a restaurant; it's a community hub where locals and visitors alike come together to enjoy delicious food and friendly service.

At Kickin Burgers, we take pride in crafting the perfect burger experience. Our juicy, handcrafted burgers are made with the finest ingredients and cooked to perfection, ensuring every bite is bursting with flavor. Whether you're craving a classic cheeseburger or feeling adventurous with our specialty creations, we have something to satisfy every palate.

But we're not just about burgers - our crispy, golden fries are the perfect accompaniment to any meal. Hand-cut and perfectly seasoned, they're the ultimate sidekick to our delicious burgers.

So whether you're stopping by for a quick bite on your lunch break or gathering with friends and family for a delicious meal, make Kicken Burgers your go-to destination for unforgettable food and unbeatable hospitality. We can't wait to welcome you!
